How to get your ad after donating.

So you just proved that you donated and you want your ad
So how do you do that?

Step 1

First press the Windows key + R (Win + R) and type "mspaint" then hit enter

Step 2

Your now in mspaint click resize

Step 3

Click Pixels

Step 4

Uncheck Maintain Aspect Ratio

Step 5

Set Horizontal to 1080 and Vertical to 163 and click OK

Step 6

Make your banner image

Step 7

"Go to File > Save As"

Step 8

Name the file "cliftonbanner"

Step 9

Email the file to cliftondonations@gmail.com
